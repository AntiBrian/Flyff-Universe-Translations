#cff0000ffKalgas Assault#nc
Two teams shall clash against each other to take control of the mighty boss 'Kalgas'
Take down the artifacts guarding 'Kalgas' to strike 'Kalgas' even harder to win the Assault!!
Various rewards are awaiting you!!

#cff0000ffRule#nc
1. Capturing an artifact gives a buff that deals additional damage to Kalgas based on the number of artifacts captured. 
2. If you spend less than a certain amount of time in the game, you will be excluded from the rewards.
3. The team that deals more damage to Kalgas and have a higher rate by the end of assault wins.
However, if one team's rate exceed 80% and they hold that status for a minute, the event ends early and the team wins.
4. Winning priority : Rate > Number of captured artifacts > PK kills between teams 
5. Even if Kalgas Assault begins without an opposing team that enters, if no damage is dealt to Kalgas, it will end as a draw.

#cff0000ffRewards#nc
Participation rewards  – 5x Kalgas Chip
Winning Team  – 10x Kalgas Chip
1st for each category – 30x Kalgas Chip
2nd for each category – 20x Kalgas Chip
3rd for each category – 15x Kalgas Chip

※ In the case of draw, only participation rewards and rewards for category ranking will be given, and no winner rewards will be given.
