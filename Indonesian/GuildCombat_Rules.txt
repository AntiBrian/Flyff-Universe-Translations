#b#cff0000ff* Basic Rules#nb#nc
- Every party and buff are canceled after the entry.
- Item trading is banned from 5 minutes before the combat.
- Combat progresses for 10 minutes.
- 5 members of advance party join first.
- The next character joins after a character died.
- The dead character becomes the last in the list.
- A character has 3 chances of revival.
* Guild Master: Gets bonus point when defeating the enemy.
* Defender(except for Guild Master): Gives bonus point to the enemy when dying.
