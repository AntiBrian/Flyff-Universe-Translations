#b Race Information #nb
Rainbow Race is an opportunity for all players to compete in an interactive and intense flying race. Players must use their own flying mount during the race.

#iNote: The application fee cannot be refunded. Each player is responsible for providing themselves with a flying mount.#ni

Rewards:
First Place: Title, Mount, and Rainbow Race First Place Box.
Second Place: Rainbow Race Second Place Box.
Third Place: Rainbow Race Third Place Box.
Fourth to Tenth Place: Rainbow Race Honorable Box.
The Rest: Participation Reward.
